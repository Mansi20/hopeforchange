SERVICES

EDUCATION

Our education system is a fully volunteer-run non-profit service whose mission is to catalyze socio-economic change in India through the education of the children who actually needs it.You all can help us in our mission by volunteering in our program.Let your knowledge help hundreds of other people.Experience the joy of volunteering!

RESOURCES

Ever thought of those old bundle of books,notes,supplemantry materials and other stuffs that are carefreely sold to the scrapdealer for few bucks. Can light up a life of the childrens who actually need it but can't afford to have it.Be the ray of hope of someone's life.

GET EXPOSED

Meet new people daily.Show the world the talent you have maybe you get someone who want to work with you or someone you wanted to work with not only that also get certified for your skills.That will be eventually add up to your resume.

NO RESTRICTIONS

As youngsters we know the importance of time and work.This initative does not put any restrictions on the time, come whenever you feel like there is no limit on the time as well as on the field.Choose any field of your choice sports,science,art & craft and anything you feel you can spread knowledge of!  It's your classroom ,let you take control of the things.

EVENTS AND PROGRAMS

Our team is going to organize various events and programs with mutual decisions in which not only the childrens but the volunteers will be also participating to encourage healthy competition and bringing out the creativity inside us.

BE THE LEADER

Leadership is not about a title or a designation. It's about impact, influence and inspiration.There will be no designation in our organization we all are leaders , we all are working for a cause, we all can give ideas, opinions for the betterment of this organization.

ABOUT US


We are a group of young professionals came together to set up this organization HOPE-For Change.Our mission is to create a platform for the professionals,students,teachers,coaches and other people who want to spread their ideas,knowledge and more importantly their talent to the childrens or the youngsters who wanted to learn it.
